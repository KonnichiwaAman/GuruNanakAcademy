We are thrilled to announce the commencement of the new academic year 2024-25 at Guru Nanak Academy. As we embark on this new journey together, we look forward to a year filled with academic excellence, personal growth, and memorable experiences.

## What's New This Year

This academic year brings several exciting changes and improvements to enhance the learning experience for our students:

### Enhanced Curriculum

We have updated our curriculum to incorporate the latest educational best practices and technologies. Our focus remains on developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills in our students.

### New Facilities

We are proud to introduce our newly upgraded computer laboratory with state-of-the-art equipment. The library has also been expanded with new books and digital resources.

### Extra-Curricular Activities

This year, we are introducing new clubs and activities including:

- Robotics Club
- Environmental Conservation Society
- Digital Art Workshop
- Public Speaking Forum
