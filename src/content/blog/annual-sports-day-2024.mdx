The much-awaited Annual Sports Day 2024 was held on February 15th at our school grounds. The event was a grand celebration of physical fitness, competitive spirit, and camaraderie among students.

## Event Highlights

The day began with a colorful march past by all four houses - **Bhai Gurdas**, **Bhai Nand Lal**, **Bhai Mani Singh**, and **Bhai Kanhaiya**. The parade was followed by the lighting of the sports torch and the oath-taking ceremony.

### Track Events

The track events witnessed fierce competition across various age categories:

- 100m Sprint
- 200m Race
- 400m Race
- 4x100m Relay
- 800m Long Distance

### Field Events

Students also participated in various field events including:

- Long Jump
- High Jump
- Shot Put
- Discus Throw
- Javelin Throw
