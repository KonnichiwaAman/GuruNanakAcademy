The Annual Science Exhibition 2024 was held on January 20th, showcasing the innovative spirit and scientific curiosity of our students. The exhibition featured over 50 projects from students across Classes V to XII.

## Exhibition Theme

This year's theme was **"Science for Sustainable Future"**, encouraging students to explore solutions for environmental challenges and sustainable development.

## Category Winners

### Senior Category (Classes IX-XII)

**First Prize**: *Solar-Powered Water Purification System*
- Team: Harpreet Singh & Jasleen Kaur (Class XI)
- A working model demonstrating affordable water purification using solar energy

**Second Prize**: *Smart Agriculture Monitoring System*
- Team: Amandeep Singh (Class XII)
- IoT-based system for monitoring soil moisture and automating irrigation

**Third Prize**: *Biodegradable Packaging from Agricultural Waste*
- Team: Navneet Kaur & Gurleen Singh (Class X)
- Eco-friendly packaging material made from rice husk and corn starch

### Junior Category (Classes V-VIII)

**First Prize**: *Model Rainwater Harvesting System*
- Team: Class VII students
- Detailed model showing rainwater collection and filtration

**Second Prize**: *Plant-Based Air Purifier*
- Team: Class VI students
- Living wall concept for indoor air purification

**Third Prize**: *Simple Hydroelectric Generator*
- Team: Class VIII students
- Working model generating electricity from water flow

## Special Exhibits

### Physics Section
- Working telescope models
- Electromagnetic demonstrations
- Sound and wave experiments

### Chemistry Section
- pH indicators from natural sources
- Crystallization displays
- Safe chemical reaction demonstrations

### Biology Section
- Plant tissue culture samples
- Human body systems models
- Ecosystem dioramas

1. **Dr. Rajinder Singh** - Professor of Physics, Delhi University

## Skills Developed

Through this exhibition, students developed:

- **Research Skills**: Gathering and analyzing information
- **Critical Thinking**: Problem-solving and hypothesis testing
- **Presentation Skills**: Explaining complex concepts simply
- **Teamwork**: Collaborating effectively with peers
- **Creativity**: Innovative approaches to common problems
